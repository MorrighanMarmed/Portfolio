Creative Graphic & UX Design

Creative graphic and UX design revolves around putting the user at the center of the design process. Understanding the target audience, their needs, preferences, and behaviors is crucial. My design decisions are based on empathy and usability testing to ensure the final product meets the users' expectations and enhances their experience.

I pay close attention to visual hierarchy and consistency in both graphic and UX design. This involves organizing elements in a way that guides the user's attention and makes the interface easy to navigate. Consistent use of colors, typography, and visual elements helps establish a cohesive brand identity and improves the overall user experience.

With the increasing use of mobile devices and the importance of accessibility, I create designs that are responsive and accessible across different screen sizes and devices. This involves employing techniques such as responsive layouts, scalable graphics, and designing with accessibility standards like WCAG (Web Content Accessibility Guidelines) in mind to ensure inclusivity for all users, including those with disabilities.
